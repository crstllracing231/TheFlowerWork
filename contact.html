Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place orders through Email, Messenger, Social Media DMs, fill out our contact form above, or order here on our website for fixed items. We'll confirm availability and delivery details with you!

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Orders placed before 12:00 PM may qualify for same-day delivery in Metro Manila and QC. During peak seasons (Valentine's, Mother's Day), we recommend ordering in advance.

What is your delivery coverage?

We primarily serve Metro Manila and Quezon City. Delivery to other nearby areas may be arranged upon request. Contact us to check if we can deliver to your location!

How fresh are your flowers?

We source fresh flowers weekly from trusted suppliers. Each arrangement is made to order, ensuring maximum freshness. With proper care, our bouquets typically last 5-7 days or more!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers and GCash. Payment details will be confirmed when you place your order.
