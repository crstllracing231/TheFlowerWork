About The Flower Work

Blooming from Passion to Business

The Flower Work began with a simple love for flowers. What started as creating arrangements for friends' birthdays and special events soon blossomed into something bigger.

I discovered that many people were searching for beautiful, aesthetic bouquets that didn't break the bank especially students and young professionals looking to make someone's day special.

That's when I realized: why not turn this passion into a business? The Flower Work was born from the desire to make quality, fresh flowers accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.

What drives me every day is the joy I see in people's faces when they receive or give our bouquets. Each arrangement tells a story, celebrates a moment, and creates a memory and I'm honored to be part of those special occasions.

Meet the Maker

Hi! I'm Iya, the heart and hands behind The Flower Work. My journey with flowers started long before this became a business, it's been a lifelong passion that has shaped who I am today.

What started as a hobby of arranging flowers for friends' birthdays quickly turned into something more meaningful. I realized there was a real need for beautiful, affordable bouquets, especially among students and young couples who wanted to give something special without overspending.

When I'm not arranging flowers, you'll find me exploring new floral trends on Instagram, visiting local flower markets early in the morning to handpick the freshest blooms, or planning the future Flower Work studio, a creative space where people can see the magic happen!
